Stand by the Jinan River, and spread my arms.


When you are finding a way home,it does not mean you have reached the end of the road,rather, it is a reminder that it is time for us to take a turn.


Now, when we drink,and clink the glasses,we hear but the sounds of them breaking.


In the talk and laughter,who would recall with nostalgia the place we desert with a broken heart?


I would like to trade a decade of my life,for a hug.


This is a great world,which is worth our excitement.


Destination—the South Pole.


When you decide to start out,you have overcome the most difficult part.


Put on headphones, "Life's a Struggle".


"


When I opened my eyes and entered this world,


My mom gave me life, but now I am left to my own devices,


This gives me fear, and in my eyes, everybody is wearing a mask.


"


END
