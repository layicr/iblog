Climbing mountains and obeying the will of the world, I looked around and bowed to heaven and earth, again and again, leaving my mind flying. In the dreams, no matter who that love-struck person is, you and I are all passersby who cannot cross the ocean and pursue the moon in the mirror.


Hunan . Ancient Town of Fenghuang
