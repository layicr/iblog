Slept on the sofa for a whole half year,
Playing Wu Bai's songs in the lobby every day,
Got complained by the brothers for a long time.

We didn't get concert tickets, but we got train tickets,
Finally just grabbed a small stool and rushed all the way to the venue outside,


Awesome,
Spent 200 yuan to go to Wu Bai's concert and sing Wu Bai's songs to Wu Bai outside the venue,
The biggest regret in life,
Is to properly hear Wu Bai properly sing,,,
