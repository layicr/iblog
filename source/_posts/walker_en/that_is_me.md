Busy with life all these years,


I would also write some words, or take some photos,


hoping to publish a book of my own, with both text and pictures


Simple, true, no cheating,


Only verses casually jotted down, with photos taken during all these years.


It is not easy to realize it, though.


My servers got moved from country to country,


My blog has been banned several times in the past three years.


Emm…


Let me count…eight times altogether.


What should I say?


Did my words or photos reflect harsh reality too much?


I have no so-called original aspiration.


Maybe I just want to be in a relationship with my dreams.


Sometimes I should stop,


Say sorry, and smile it away.


I can be carefree, and let it be.


No matter how others may disdain me,


I wander around unrestrained, worrying about neither losses nor gains.


I do not disappoint any encounter in life. That is me,


A psycho.


END


Shanghai
