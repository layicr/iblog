After the work overtime on weekends，


Walk into an obscure kerbside diner ，


Say Hi to the owner，


Order a combo meal，


With a soy milk，


Seated beside the stereo，


The song from the band May Day again，


I always think of myself，


An unqualified May Day fan，


Although I've been to their live concert in Xi'an Stadium，


I did not sing along till the last song like surrounding everyone else，


Sitting there quietly to listen，


Tens of thousands of audience choired LOVE-ing，


We all turned on the cell phone screen to sprinkle the TENDERNESS，


ENCORE and encore again to the last song，


In the ETERNAL MID-SUMMER no one else can feel，


Please do not disrupt，


Such softness，


I took out a bottle of RIO from my pocket，


The rain ceased when I finished my drink，


Alcohol lingering around my head，


I walked along the street，


Suddenly a breeze arrived，


The whole world turned vivid，


There was only me left alone in a wink。


END


Nanning Wuxu International Airport
